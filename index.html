About Us

At Coffee & Friends, we believe that great coffee is just the beginning. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where people can connect, relax, and enjoy the finer things in life.

Located at the beautiful Showcase on Floor G in Saint James's, we source only the highest quality beans and ingredients to bring you an unforgettable experience. Whether you're here for a quick morning boost or a long afternoon chat, you're always among friends.
